About Us

FrontlineForces aims to help people affected by natural or man made calamities. We are always there to help you!!!

  • We track the location of the person affected.

  • People can donate money/clothes or anything they wish to donate to the affected people.
  • We hold webinars to make people understand the precautions to be taken for every calamity.
  • We have a chatbot as well wherein people can just select a word and they will recieve help.



Learn More






Our Mission and Strategic Objectives

FrontlineForces has main priorities for the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals and the improvement of the human condition and helping in natural disasters.

It is the minds of men and women that the defences of peace must be constructed.

FrontlineForces uses education, science, culture, communication and information to foster mutual understanding and respect for our planet.
